Membership FAQ

Clear & Transparent Billing

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes, entirely risk-free. You can cancel your recurring subscription with one click inside your dashboard. You will retain access until the end of your billing cycle.

Can I switch my primary game?

Absolutely. Your AimForge subscription gives you full access to all coaches and VODs across all supported games (Valorant, CS2, Apex, etc.).

What happens if I miss a live session?

We require 24 hours notice for rescheduling. Unused live coaching hours rollover to the next month as long as your subscription remains active.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No hidden fees, no lock-in contracts. You only pay for the month (or year, if you selected the discounted annual plan) you are actively using.

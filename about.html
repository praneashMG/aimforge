About AimForge

Elevating the competitive standards of gaming through expert coaching, data-driven methodology, and an unstoppable community.

Our Story in Esports

From Passionate Players to Global Leaders

AimForge was born out of a simple frustration: climbing the competitive ladder shouldn't require guessing. Founded in 2022 by former professional players, we noticed a massive gap between casual tutorials and genuine, professional-grade coaching.

What started as a small Discord server offering free VOD reviews quickly exploded into a fully-fledged academy. Today, AimForge is home to thousands of aspiring athletes aiming to perfect their crosshair placement, utility usage, and macro-level decision making.

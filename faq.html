Platform & Accounts

Getting Started on AimForge

Is AimForge free to use?

Creating an account and joining our community Discord is 100% free. Free users also get access to our basic mechanics guides and written articles. Premium features like VOD reviews, live 1-on-1s, and Pro Masterclasses require an active subscription.

How do I link my game accounts?

Navigate to your User Dashboard and click on "Integrations". From there, you can securely link your Riot ID, Steam Account, or EA Account. This allows our platform to automatically pull your match stats for our coaches to analyze.

Can I change my primary game later?

Yes. While you select a primary game during registration, you can switch your focus at any time in your profile settings. Your subscription gives you access to training materials and coaches across all our supported titles.

Coaching & VODs

Learning the Meta

How do I submit a VOD for review?

Upload your unlisted video to YouTube and paste the link into the "Submit VOD" section of your dashboard. Please ensure the video is at least 720p/60fps and includes your in-game comms.

Do coaches actually play matches with me?

We do not offer "boosting" or carry services. During a "Live 1-on-1" session, you will stream your screen via Discord while the coach watches and provides real-time feedback, macro calls, and post-round analysis.

Can I request a specific coach?

Yes, Pro Tier subscribers can select their preferred coach when booking a live session, subject to that coach's availability and calendar.

Subscriptions & Payments

Managing your membership

What is the difference between Plus and Pro?

AimForge Plus gives you access to the complete Video Library and 2 asynchronous VOD reviews per month. AimForge Pro includes everything in Plus, but adds 4 live 1-on-1 coaching hours per month and priority queueing for team scrims.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to Dashboard > Settings > Billing. Your premium access will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you find the platform isn't helping you improve within your first week, simply contact support for a full refund.

Technical Support

Troubleshooting issues

Why isn't my Discord role updating?

If you've recently upgraded your subscription but don't see your new role in Discord, go to your AimForge Dashboard, disconnect your Discord account, and re-authorize it. The bot will refresh your permissions immediately.

The video player is buffering constantly.

Our Masterclasses are hosted in 4K resolution. If you're experiencing buffering, click the gear icon on the player and lower the resolution to 1080p or 720p. Also, ensure your browser hardware acceleration is enabled.
